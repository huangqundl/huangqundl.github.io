Bio

I am a researcher at Huawei Future Network Lab. I got my Ph.D. degree in July 2015 in Department of Computer Science and Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong, advised by Professor Patrick P. C. Lee. I received my bachelor degree in Computer Science from Peking University in 2011.
Research interests:
My research mainly focuses on distributed stream processing and network measurement. I'm also interested in areas including distributed systems, computer networks, operating systems, cloud computing, randomized algorithms and machine learning.
Currently, I lead Network Analytic Project in Huawei Future Network Lab, which aims to build high-performance and robust architectures for network analytics with recent advances in SDN and Big Data systems.
